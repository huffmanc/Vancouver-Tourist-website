We intend to build a user friendly, easy to navigate website to promote visiting Vancouver. Our focus is to bring tourists and travelers into the city through any means, so hotels and Airbnb are both good choices in our opinion. We will be trying to help Airbnb get more recognition and be seen as a viable alternative but not a replacement for hotels. We believe that all business is good business and that both Airbnb and hotels support Vancouver’s economy. Our website will utilize user friendly charts and maps to demonstrate all travel options for potential Airbnb users.
