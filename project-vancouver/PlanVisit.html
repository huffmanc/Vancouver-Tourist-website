Getting Here

Air

If you're arriving by air, you'll most likely be flying into Vancouver International Airport (YVR). Located just 25 minutes from downtown, YVR is consistently rated one of the best airports in North America. Air Canada is your best choice to Vancouver with the most non-stop flights and access from over 190 cities worldwide.

Another great place to check for affordable and timely flights is Google Flights

Car

Another alternative, is to travel by car. This will allow you to set your own schedule and witness all of Vancouver's breathtaking beauty as you drive in.

For information on road conditions within BC, contact BC Ministry of Transportation at 604.660.9770 (Vancouver) or visit www.th.gov.bc.ca

Water

Since Vancouver sits next to water, another convenient way to travel to Vancouver is by water.

BC Ferries provides regularly scheduled passenger and vehicle service between Vancouver, Vancouver Island, the Gulf Islands and other parts of coastal British Columbia.
